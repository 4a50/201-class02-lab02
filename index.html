Been doing it as a hobby since my mother brought home an IBM XT. I love to learn and am interested in almost anything people are to show me. I do have an associate degree in Technical Studies, as well as a ton of management experience, was in the Navy for 20 years. Some goals that I am striving for, is to become a paid Software Developer.
